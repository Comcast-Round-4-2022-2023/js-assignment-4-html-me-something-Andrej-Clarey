My Evolution of the Dish

In my first attempt at creating this dish I thought back to when i had seen it made by my Grandmother. When she made it she had added cherry tomatoes to the garlic, butter and olive oil. My wife, Amy, was not a fan of the acidity the tomatoes added so i ended up removing them in later versions. After making the dish a few times more I felt like it was missing something. Initally I added shallots and freshly shredded parmsean cheese. While the shallott!!!!!. Through trial and error i have created Amy's favorite pasta dish!
